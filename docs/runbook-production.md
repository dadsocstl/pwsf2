# Production Runbook

## Operations checklist
- Confirm cluster and platform service health.
- Verify policy enforcement and admission controls.
- Confirm logging, metrics, and alerting are active.
- Review backup and recovery status.

## Incident response baseline
1. Capture impact and affected components.
2. Isolate unsafe workloads or namespaces.
3. Collect evidence from logs and audit trails.
4. Execute approved remediation and recovery steps.
